ECLYPS

This time 9 years ago, Eclypse Studio was just a pen, a drawing board, and an idea in three young minds of students. At their class table, they sketched out their first designs, and the future of Eclypse Studio. Since then, they have not stopped moving, and have no intention of stopping. Fast forward 13 years and they are operating from three international offices and designing in Albania,Kosovo,Macedonia.
